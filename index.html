About Me

Latest Thinking

The Art of Semantic HTML

Published: April 24, 2026

Modern web development isn't just about making things look good. It's about creating a rock-solid document structure that search engines can parse and screen readers can understand. Without semantic HTML, a website is just a house of cards in a hurricane.

Why Security is Not an Afterthought

Security isn't a feature you can bolt on at the end. A single missing `rel="noopener"` on an external link can expose your users to phishing attacks. A missing `sandbox` on an iframe can give an attacker control of the entire page. True frontend mastery means thinking like a guardian from the very first `` tag.
